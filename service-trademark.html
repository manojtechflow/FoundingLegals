Trademark Registration

Protect your brand identity with trademark registration

What is Trademark Registration?

A trademark is a distinctive sign, symbol, word, or phrase that identifies and distinguishes your products or services from others. Trademark registration provides legal protection against unauthorized use and strengthens your brand identity.

Types of Trademarks

Word Mark

Protection for specific words, names, slogans, or phrases.

Logo/Device Mark

Protection for visual symbols, designs, or logos.

Composite Mark

Combination of words and logos together.

Service Mark

Similar to trademark but for services.

Why Register Your Trademark?

  • Exclusive legal rights to use the mark nationwide
  • Protection against infringement and unauthorized use
  • Legal recourse to sue infringers for damages
  • Valuable intangible business asset
  • Enhanced brand credibility and trust
  • Facilitates franchise and licensing opportunities
  • Use of Â® symbol after registration
  • Valid for 10 years with renewal option

Documents Required

  • Applicant details (Individual/Company/LLP)
  • PAN Card and address proof
  • Certificate of Incorporation (for companies)
  • Logo/word mark in JPG format
  • List of goods/services to be covered
  • Date of first use (if applicable)
