Import Export Code (IEC)

Enable international trade with IEC registration for global market access

What is Import Export Code?

Import Export Code (IEC) is a unique 10-digit code issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) to Indian companies for conducting import and export business. It is mandatory for any business entity looking to engage in international trade of goods and services.

Why Do You Need IEC?

  • Mandatory for importing or exporting goods and services
  • Required for availing export benefits and duty drawback
  • Needed for claiming input tax credit on imports under GST
  • Essential for opening foreign currency accounts
  • Required for participating in international exhibitions
  • Lifetime validity with no renewal required

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the entity
  • Aadhaar Card of proprietor/partners/directors
  • Certificate of Incorporation (for companies/LLPs)
  • Address proof of business premises
  • Bank certificate or cancelled cheque
  • Photograph of applicant/authorized signatory
