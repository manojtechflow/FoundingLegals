Our Story

Who We Are

FoundingLegals is a premier legal and compliance service provider dedicated to supporting freshly founded startups in India. We understand that navigating the complex landscape of legal requirements can be overwhelming for new entrepreneurs.

Our mission is to simplify the legal journey for startups by providing comprehensive, affordable, and efficient solutions. From company incorporation to obtaining mandatory certifications, we handle all the legal complexities so you can focus on building your business.

With a team of experienced legal professionals and industry experts, we've successfully helped over 500 startups achieve full legal compliance and unlock their growth potential.
