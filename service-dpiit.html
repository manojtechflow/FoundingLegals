What is DPIIT Recognition?

DPIIT (Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade) Recognition certifies your entity as a startup under the Government of India's Startup India initiative. This recognition provides numerous benefits including tax exemptions, easier compliance, access to government schemes, and credibility with investors.

Important: DPIIT certification requires an Org DSC (Organisation Digital Signature Certificate). This is obtained as part of your Founding Services package â€” ensure Org DSC is in place before initiating DPIIT application.

Benefits of DPIIT Recognition

Tax Benefits

Income tax exemption for 3 consecutive years out of first 10 years

IPR Benefits

80% rebate on patent filing and 50% rebate on trademark filing

Self Certification

Self-certify compliance under 9 labor and environment laws

Easy Funding

Access to Fund of Funds and government funding schemes

Government Tenders

Exemption from prior experience/turnover criteria

Networking

Access to startup events, incubators, and opportunities

Eligibility Criteria

  • Entity should be a Private Limited Company, LLP, or Partnership Firm
  • Should be incorporated for less than 10 years
  • Annual turnover should not exceed â‚¹100 crores
  • Working towards innovation or improvement of products/services
  • Should not be formed by splitting up existing business

Documents Required

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Details of Directors/Partners with PAN & Aadhaar
  • Brief description of business and innovation
  • Website URL (if available)
  • Patent/Trademark details (if any)
