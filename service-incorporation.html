Auditor Appointment

First auditor appointment within 30 days of incorporation as mandated under Section 139 of the Companies Act, 2013. We prepare and file Form ADT-1 with MCA.

7

GST Registration

Goods & Services Tax registration on the GST portal. Includes document verification, application filing, ARN tracking, and GSTIN certificate delivery.

8

GST Monthly Filings

Ongoing monthly GST return filing management (GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B). We ensure timely, accurate filings to avoid penalties and maintain compliance.

9

Org DSC (Organisation Digital Signature)

Organisation-level DSC required for DPIIT certification, advanced MCA filings, and official government submissions on behalf of the company entity.

10

Directors Meeting Appointment

First Board Meeting conducted within 30 days of incorporation as required under the Companies Act. We prepare the notice, agenda, and minutes documentation.

11

Tax Returns

Annual Income Tax Return filing for your company (ITR-6). Includes preparation, review, and e-filing with the Income Tax Department of India.
