Company Incorporation

Register your Private Limited Company, LLP, or One Person Company with complete legal compliance

What is Company Incorporation?

Company incorporation is the legal process of forming a corporate entity or company. A company incorporated in India enjoys several benefits including limited liability, separate legal entity status, perpetual succession, and easier access to funding.

Types of Company Registration

Private Limited Company

Most popular for startups seeking VC funding. Limited liability with 2-200 shareholders.

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Best for professional services. Hybrid structure with partnership flexibility.

One Person Company (OPC)

Ideal for solo entrepreneurs. Enjoy company benefits with single ownership.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of all directors/partners
  • Aadhaar Card of all directors/partners
  • Address proof (Passport/Voter ID/Driving License)
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Proof of registered office address
  • Rental agreement or ownership documents
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from property owner
