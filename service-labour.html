Labour Licences

Obtain necessary labour licenses for complete legal compliance

What are Labour Licences?

Labour licenses are mandatory registrations required under various labour laws in India. These licenses ensure compliance with employment regulations and protect the rights of both employers and employees.

Types of Labour Licences

Shops & Establishment

Mandatory for all commercial establishments. Regulates working hours and conditions.

Contract Labour License

Required when employing 20+ contract workers.

Factory License

Needed for manufacturing units with 10+ workers.

PF & ESI Registration

Mandatory social security registrations for employees.

Professional Tax

State-level tax on professions and employment.

Labour Welfare Fund

State-specific employee welfare fund contributions.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of the business entity
  • Certificate of Incorporation/Partnership Deed
  • Address proof of business premises
  • Rent agreement or ownership documents
  • List of employees with details
  • Bank account details
