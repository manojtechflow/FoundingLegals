Food safety license for food businesses and manufacturers

What is FSSAI License?

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) license is mandatory for any business involved in food manufacturing, processing, packaging, distribution, or selling. It ensures food safety standards and builds consumer trust.

Who Needs FSSAI License?

  • Food manufacturers and processors
  • Restaurants, cafes, and cloud kitchens
  • Food packaging and labeling companies
  • Food storage and warehousing
  • Food importers and exporters
  • Food retailers and distributors
  • Catering services and food delivery

Documents Required

  • Form B application (duly filled)
  • PAN Card of business entity
  • Certificate of Incorporation/Partnership Deed
  • List of food products to be manufactured/sold
  • Layout plan of processing unit
  • Address proof of business premises
